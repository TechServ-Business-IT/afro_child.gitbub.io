afro child. by Shernice Dixion

afro child. is a an independent brand founded by Shernice Dixion, a resident of Eldorado Park and proud mommy of one energetic son. Like him too, she is enthusiastic about hair and if you have a moment to spare - could learn some valuable tips and tricks to treating and caring for your natural hair.

afro child. was started as a lifestyle movement to raise awareness around the downplayed beauty of natural hair, body shapes and homeopathic remedies and hacks for body, mind and hair follicle!

Tried and tested by the founder herself, you can bet that recommendations and suggestions made by afro child. are sure to leave you hairper than before!
